About Us


    Touch Tap, LLC was created by Sherri Martinez.  Sherri started teaching at Nimitz Elementary in August, 1994 as a Special Education Teacher.

She earned a Master’s Degree in Educational Psychology from the University of Hawaii at Manoa.  Sherri started as a Teacher of Visually Impaired Students

in August, 1999 in Central District.  She earned Master’s Degrees in Special Education: Vision and Orientation and Mobility from the University of Northern Colorado.

Touch Tap, LLC was created to provide services to adults with visual impairment and help make visually impaired people feel confident while being independent.

Independence varies by each individual, and Sherri is prepared to assist with that path to independence.  Let’s chat and set a course to your independence!

Sherri is an official Hawaii Transcriber of UEB, Unified English Braille, via The Library of Congress course administrated by The National Federation of the Blind.

Books and materials can be transcribed into contracted and uncontracted Braille.
